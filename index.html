Organ Donation

WHAT IS ORGAN DONATION?

Organ Donation is the gift of an organ to a
person with end stage organ disease and who
needs a transplant. The donated organ may be
from a deceased donor or a living donor.

WHAT IS TRANSPLANTATION?

Surgical operation in which a failing or
damaged organ in the human body is removed
and replaced with a functioning one.

WHO CAN PLEDGE TO DONATE ORGANS?

Any person not less than 18 years of age, who voluntarily authorizes the removal of any of his organ and/or tissue…
